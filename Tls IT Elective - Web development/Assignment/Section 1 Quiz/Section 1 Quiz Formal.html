7) Summary: Structuring a Website

  • Use clear headings for each section.
  • Organize content logically (text, links, images, lists, tables).
  • Apply consistent, basic styling and color.
  • Include helpful internal links (bookmarks) on longer pages.
  • Respect copyright and give attribution when needed.
